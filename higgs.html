Gravitational waves from the Higgs field

Rice University | Supervised by Dr. Andrew Long

Project Overview

We calculate the gravitational wave energy density power spectrum resulting from the relaxation of the Higgs field in various eras of the universe.

Results

While gravitational waves resulting from the relaxation of the Higgs field in the radiation dominated era are undetectable, GW due to the GUT field are certainly powerful enough to be registered by contemporary GW detectors.
