Chess

I like to play chess! When I'm winning, at least. I found chess to be a very brutal game. Wins and losses are dependent solely on your own skill level relative to your opponent. This is nice in the sense that you don't have to rely on luck - though I suppose there's luck in who you get matched up with. Though, the absence of all variables except your own chess aptitude puts a lot of exciting pressure to perform. Here's a less embarassing game of mine from January 17, 2022. I had almost perfect play in a 3+2 (!) time format.
